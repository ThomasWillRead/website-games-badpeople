GETTING STARTED

The object of the game is to be the first player to score 7 points. Laughing, crying, screaming, drinking, and being a jerk is not only accepted - it’s encouraged!


GAME PLAY

STEP #1: The last person to arrive begins as the Dictator. The Dictator draws a Question Card and reads it out loud.

STEP #2: The Dictator casts their vote by silently choosing one of the players.

STEP #3: Each player must also cast a silent vote by guessing who the Dictator voted for.

STEP #4: Once every player has voted, each player must then reveal their vote one-by-one starting with the player to the left of the Dictator.

STEP #5: After the scores have been written down, play another round! The player to the left of the Dictator becomes the new Dictator.


SCORING

1 point is given to each player who voted the same as the Dictator. If the Dictator chooses a player that no-one voted for, then everyone who voted for the most popular answer receives 1 point.
